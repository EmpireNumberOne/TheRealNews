What's with the name change at The Villas at Mountain Vista?
Have you noticed a name change at The Villas at Mountain Vista? The new name is 5400 Vistas. I wonder why an apartment owner would change the name of the apartment? I made the mistake of renting an apartment at the Villas at Mountain Vista. I noticed a pattern similar to what I experienced at Mesa Verde Apartments. The rent on the lease was $947, but the Villas at Mountain Vista informed me that a payment of $1890 was due on June 22, 2017. When I refused to pay, I was evicted by the constable on June 28, 2017. Funny how an apartment can request $1890 on an apartment that is listed at $947 and the police backs up the apartment. I am 0 for 3 in my experience. My debt was assigned to National Credit Systems for $4,322.42. My dream of buying a house was in the hole thanks to renting apartments in Vegas. I should have rented a house. I know what my readers are thinking and I have the answers. The Villas at Mountain Vista kept my $400 deposit and charged me an outrageous cleaning fee of $1090.
